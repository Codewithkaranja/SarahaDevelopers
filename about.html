Who We Are

Saraha Developers is a modern software development company delivering high-performance digital solutions for businesses across Nairobi and beyond.

Founded with a mission to empower African businesses through technology, Saraha Developers specializes in professional website design, custom software development, e-commerce platforms, mobile applications, and enterprise digital systems.

We design and engineer solutions that are fast, secure, scalable, and strategically aligned with business goals. Our work supports organizations in healthcare, education, construction, hospitality, government, NGOs, startups, and established enterprises.

At Saraha Developers, we don’t just build websites — we build digital infrastructure that drives efficiency, credibility, and long-term growth.
