2) Google Business Profile optimization (fastest wins)

Your GBP is your Athi River “sales card” on Google. Get the setup right and keep it active.

Primary category: choose the closest match to your main service (not a generic category).
Services list: add your top 10–20 services with simple names.
Description: explain what you do + Athi River naturally (avoid keyword stuffing).
Photos weekly: real work, team, tools, before/after, branding.
Posts: 1 post per week (tip, offer, recent job, update).

3) Reviews: your strongest local SEO weapon

Reviews help you rank and also close customers faster. In a smaller market, consistent reviews make you look “bigger.”

Weekly review goal: 2–5 reviews/week for most niches (more if very competitive).
Ask at the right moment: immediately after a successful job or delivery.
Encourage detail: “Mention the service and Athi River.”
Reply to all: thank them + mention the service naturally.
Copy/paste review request (WhatsApp):
“Asante for choosing us! If you have 30 seconds, please leave a Google review. It helps other customers in Athi River find a trusted service: [paste review link]”

4) Build a local landing page for Athi River

If you want to rank consistently, your website should support your GBP. Create a dedicated page targeting Athi River instead of hiding everything on the homepage.

Page title: “Service in Athi River” (e.g., “Plumbing in Athi River”).
Sections: benefits, process, pricing guidance, FAQs, gallery, CTA.
Internal links: link to your main service page and contact page.
Schema: LocalBusiness/Service schema to help Google understand the location.

5) Citations & NAP consistency (Athi River)

Citations are online mentions of your business name, address, and phone number (NAP). Consistency helps Google trust your profile and reduces ranking volatility.

Same phone number everywhere: website, Facebook, directories, invoices, business cards.
Same business name formatting: don’t add/remove keywords across platforms.
Same address rules: if you’re service-area, don’t publish random office addresses online.

6) Mistakes that block Local SEO results

Wrong category (you rank for the wrong search intent).
No review system (competitors outgrow you quietly).
Thin website content (no local page, no FAQs, no proof).
Inconsistent NAP (Google doesn’t trust the data).
Dead profiles (no posts, no photos, no updates).

7) Athi River Local SEO checklist (quick audit)

Use this checklist weekly to improve rankings and leads.

GBP category is correct and specific.
Services list includes your top services.
Photos: 20+ real photos and add weekly.
Reviews: consistent weekly plan + replies to every review.
Posts: 1 post weekly (tip/offer/recent work).
Website: Athi River landing page with FAQs + clear CTA.
NAP: same business details across platforms.
